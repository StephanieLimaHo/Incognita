About us


We are a group of students from sophomore year - class of 2021 - of IFPR (Instituto Federal do Paraná - Campus Foz do Iguaçu), who have been developing this amazing website! In which, the purpose is to provide a platform for intermediate and advanced English speakers, who intend to improve their video interpretation and expand their vocabulary, by watching social interest educational videos. Besides watching, the user also has the possibility to practice the vocabulary and the content learnt, through listening and reading activities.

It all began as part of an evaluation method, an Active Methodology, called project-based learning (PBL) where three subjects (English, Web Development and Computer Network) had to be gathered. In order to make everything happen, we developed the website step-by-step throughout the year, collecting data and programming. So here is the final result!
